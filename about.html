History of our University

Founded in 1985 by Joshua Olape. a wealthy man from Nigeria, SSL University started as a small school. Olape used his inheritance to establish the university, aiming to provide a center for education and learning in the region.

In the 20th century, the university underwent significant reforms to modernize its curriculum and administration. And since then, it has continued to evolve while maintaining its historical and cultural significance.

JOSHUA OLAPE: Founder of SSL University

Joshua Olape was born in 1942 in a small village in Nigeria. From a young age, he displayed an exceptional talent for science and mathematics. Growing up in a modest family, Joshua's intellectual curiosity was nurtured by his parents, who valued education despite their limited means. He excelled in his studies and earned a scholarship to study engineering at a prestigious university in Lagos.

In the early 1980s, after completing his doctorate in computer science in the United States, Joshua Olape returned to Nigeria with a vision to revolutionize education in Africa. He was particularly interested in the potential of computer science and engineering to drive economic and social development. In 1985, with the support of international donors and the Nigerian government, Joshua founded SSL (Science, Software, and Learning) University.

Main Facilities

Gym

The gym at SSL University is a state-of-the-art fitness center designed to cater to students, faculty, and staff. Equipped with the latest exercise machines, free weights, and cardiovascular equipment, it offers a variety of fitness classes, including yoga, pilates, and spinning. The facility also features a swimming pool, basketball courts, and a running track, providing ample opportunities for both individual workouts and team sports. The gym’s professional trainers are available for personal training sessions and to help develop customized fitness plans.

Library

The SSL University Library is a modern, expansive facility that serves as the academic heart of the campus. It houses a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources covering a wide range of disciplines. The library features quiet study areas, group study rooms, and multimedia stations to support different study needs. Advanced research tools and databases are accessible both on-campus and remotely, aiding students in their academic pursuits. Additionally, the library offers workshops on research skills and information literacy to help students navigate and utilize its resources effectively.

Computer Lab

The computer lab at SSL University is a high-tech environment designed to support the needs of students across all disciplines. It is equipped with the latest computers and software applications relevant to various fields of study, including programming, graphic design, and data analysis. The lab provides access to high-speed internet, printing services, and specialized equipment such as 3D printers and virtual reality headsets. Experienced staff members are available to assist with technical issues and offer training sessions on new technologies and software. The computer lab is an essential resource for completing assignments, conducting research, and collaborating on projects.

Classroom

Classrooms at SSL University are designed to create an optimal learning environment. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids, including interactive whiteboards, projectors, and high-quality sound systems. The seating arrangements are flexible, allowing for both traditional lectures and group work. Wi-Fi access is available throughout the classrooms, facilitating the use of digital resources and online learning tools. Additionally, many classrooms are designed to support hybrid learning, accommodating both in-person and remote students. The focus is on creating a dynamic and engaging atmosphere that enhances the learning experience for all students.

Community

The community at SSL University is vibrant, diverse, and inclusive, fostering a sense of belonging among students, faculty, and staff. With members from various cultural and academic backgrounds, the university promotes a rich exchange of ideas and perspectives. Students actively engage in numerous clubs, organizations, and activities that cater to a wide range of interests, from academic and professional development to arts, sports, and social causes.
